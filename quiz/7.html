True Correct! In the 2022 midterm elections, Detroit voters saw ads personalized to their identities and political leaning.

True Correct! Researchers at the University of South Carolina found that targeting certain ads to people could change how they thought about themselves.

True Correct! This occurred during the 2022 midterm elections (clearly a terrible time for digital privacy).

True Correct! In 2021, a group of 204 Australian teens conducted their own research, and came up with over 5,000 unhealthy ads over a 2-week period, an average of 12.6 ads per person each week.
